Lessons

Every Weekday the Courts close at 5. lessons go from 2 or 3 to 5 depending on the day

Open Courts

Saturday is open court all day, come when you want and leave when you want, after 3 pm is clean-up.

Any extra tennis balls brought from home that have not been picked up before 3:30 may get stolen. everyone is off the courts after 3:30 and stray tennis balls are left where they're at.

Sign Up for a Lesson
This is where you can Sign up Please enter your Name, Last Name Address, E-mail, Phone Number, and the Coach you would like to have a lesson with. We ask that you give your first and last name so that the coach can have you entered in their attendance list as soon as you sign up we ask for your address so that we can mail you the schedule if you lose your schedule you can always look at our on-line table and under your coaches name that you can choose below we ask for your E-mail and Phone Number in case we need to contact you about upcoming events. You can choose your Coach based on availability if your schedule does not work out with ours please contact us, and we can form an arrangement. All fields below are required, please check to make sure all information is correct before you submit. Thank you and were looking forward to seeing you on the courts.
